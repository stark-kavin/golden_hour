Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

A method to peel back the layers of a problem using the
"5 Whys" technique.

The Pioneer

Sakichi Toyoda

Founder of Toyota Industries. He believed that by asking "Why?" five times, the nature of the problem as well as its solution becomes clear.

The Problem

"The factory welding robot stopped working."

1
Why did it stop?

The fuse blew.

A symptom of electrical failure.

2
Why did the fuse blow?

Circuit overloaded.

Too much power drawn at once.

3
Why was it overloaded?

The shaft was straining.

Mechanical resistance in the system.

4
Why was it straining?

Insufficient Lubrication.

Friction caused increased load.

5
Why was there no lubrication?

The Root Cause

Oil pump intake clogged with shavings.

Now we know we need a filter, not just a fuse.

Symptom Fix

Replace Fuse

Result: Breaks again tomorrow.

Real Fix

Attach Filter

Result: Problem Solved Forever.

Stop Fixing Symptoms.

Ask "Why?" until you find the source.
